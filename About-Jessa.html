Welcome to My World

Hi, I’m Jessa Fayer! While my full name is Jessica, I’ve always preferred Jessa—it feels more like *me*. I’m a 21-year-old Ukrainian-American who grew up in the Maryland/DC area. In 2024, I graduated with a degree in Information Science, specializing in Data Analytics. My journey has been driven by a deep love for creativity and technology.

Creative Expression Meets Technology

Education has been a gateway to creative freedom for me. I love building websites and designing virtual games, blending technical skills with artistry to create something truly unique. My expertise also extends to user experience research, where I focus on making projects visually compelling and user-friendly.

Check out my tech portfolio to see my innovative projects.

Entrepreneurial Spirit

In college, I co-founded an educational startup funded by the University of Maryland. In 2021—long before ChatGPT became mainstream—we developed an AI-powered learning assistant to help students with their homework. I was one of three original coders and took charge of branding, social media, and marketing campaigns. Our project’s success fueled my passion for combining technology with creative solutions.

Acting & Modeling

Acting has always been my first love. From theater performances as a child to participating in student films and directing my own projects in college, storytelling is a core part of who I am. My modeling journey began when I was scouted, leading me to editorial, print, and commercial work. Studying in Europe gave me the chance to model in Paris, Berlin, and Madrid, and I loved using myself as a canvas for art.

Beyond the Spotlight

When I’m not acting or modeling, I enjoy spending time with my family and dogs. I also run a small business called Jessica’s Pickle Delights, where I sell uniquely flavored pickles at farmers markets. It’s a fun passion project that combines my love of community and creativity.

Want to collaborate? Feel free to explore my portfolio and reach out!
